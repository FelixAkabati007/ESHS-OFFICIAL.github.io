Academic Information

Academic Programs

Ejisuman Senior High School offers a wide range of academic programs designed to prepare students for higher education and future careers. Our curriculum is designed to provide a balanced education that fosters intellectual, social, and personal development.

Courses & Programs Offered

Science Program

  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Elective Mathematics

The Science Program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in the natural sciences. Students will engage in hands-on laboratory experiments, research projects, and theoretical studies to develop a deep understanding of scientific principles and their applications.

Business Program

  • Business Management
  • Accounting
  • Economics
  • Elective Mathematics

The Business Program aims to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary for success in the business world. Through courses in management, accounting, economics, and mathematics, students will learn about business operations, financial analysis, and economic theory.

Arts Program

  • Literature in English
  • History
  • Geography
  • Government

The Arts Program offers students the opportunity to explore various disciplines within the humanities. Students will study literature, history, geography, and government to gain a comprehensive understanding of human culture, society, and the world around them.

Technical Program

  • Technical Drawing
  • Building Construction
  • Woodwork
  • Metalwork

The Technical Program is designed for students interested in pursuing careers in engineering, construction, and other technical fields. Students will learn technical drawing, building construction, woodwork, and metalwork through practical projects and theoretical studies.

Extracurricular Activities

At Ejisuman Senior High School, we believe in the holistic development of our students. We offer a variety of extracurricular activities to help students develop their talents and interests outside the classroom.

  • Debate Club
  • Chess Club
  • Environmental Club
  • Drama Club
  • Sports Teams
  • Music and Dance

Academic Support

We provide various academic support services to help our students succeed. These include:

  • Tutoring Programs
  • Study Groups
  • Library Resources
  • Guidance and Counseling
  • Career Counseling
