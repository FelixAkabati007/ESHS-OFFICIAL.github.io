History

Established in 1970

Ejisuman Senior High School's journey to becoming a respected institution in Ghana began with its establishment in 1970. Originally founded with a mission to provide quality education to the youth of the Ashanti Region, the school has steadily grown in reputation and size over the years.

The school continues to evolve and adapt, embracing modern teaching methods and technology to ensure students receive the best education possible. The school's success stories are a testament to its unwavering commitment to developing well-rounded individuals who go on to make significant contributions to society, both locally and internationally. Today, Ejisuman SHS stands as a beacon of excellence and a source of pride for the Ashanti Region and beyond.
