Our Crest

The crest of Ejisuman Senior High School is a symbol of our rich heritage and commitment to excellence. It embodies the values and principles that guide our institution.

The crest features a shield divided into four quadrants, each representing a core aspect of our school's identity:

  • Book and Torch: Symbolizes knowledge and enlightenment, reflecting our dedication to academic excellence.
  • Tree: Represents growth and development, signifying our commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals.
  • Handshake: Denotes unity and cooperation, highlighting the importance of community and teamwork.
  • Star: Symbolizes aspiration and achievement, encouraging our students to reach for the stars.

The motto "Knowledge, Integrity, Service" is prominently displayed on the crest, encapsulating the core values that drive our mission and vision.
