About Us

Virtual Tour

Take a virtual tour of our campus to explore our facilities and get a feel for the learning environment at Ejisuman Senior High School.

Ejisuman Senior High School's journey to becoming a respected institution in Ghana began with its establishment in 1970. Originally founded with a mission to provide quality education to the youth of the Ashanti Region, the school has steadily grown in reputation and size over the years.

The school continues to evolve and adapt, embracing modern teaching methods and technology to ensure students receive the best education possible. The school's success stories are a testament to its unwavering commitment to developing well-rounded individuals who go on to make significant contributions to society, both locally and internationally.

Academic Excellence

Ejisuman Senior High School is known for its commitment to academic excellence. Our students consistently perform well in the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE) and other national competitions. We pride ourselves on our holistic approach to education, focusing not only on academic achievement but also on character development and extracurricular activities.
