Our Facilities

Ejisuman Senior High School boasts a range of modern facilities designed to support the academic and extracurricular activities of our students.

Classrooms

Our classrooms are well-equipped with modern teaching aids and provide a conducive environment for learning. Each classroom is spacious and designed to accommodate a comfortable number of students.

Administration

The administration block at Ejisuman Senior High School is the hub for the school's operations, housing the principal's office, administrative offices, and staff rooms. It is essential for decision-making, record-keeping, and daily tasks.

ICT Laboratory

Our ICT laboratory is equipped with modern computers and internet access to support digital literacy and technology-based learning. Students have access to various software and online resources to enhance their learning experience.

Library

The school library offers a wide range of books, journals, and digital resources to support academic research and reading for pleasure. The library provides a quiet and comfortable space for students to study and explore new knowledge.

Dining Hall

The dining hall provides nutritious meals for students and staff. It is a spacious and hygienic facility where students can enjoy their meals in a comfortable setting.

Sports Facilities

Our sports facilities include a football field, basketball court, and volleyball court. These facilities support the physical development of our students and provide opportunities for sports and recreational activities.
